About JM

I am an entrepreneur with a passion for high-quality menswear, menswear education, and JavaScript Development.

In the last ten years, I have dedicated myself to small business and local economic development. I work closely with other entrepreneurs, c-level executives, and senior leadership to create unique projects that bring brand awareness and generate revenue.

Apart from being a full-time entrepreneur, I'm a husband and father to be living and working in San Antonio, Texas. I love music, menswear, business development, coffee, and a good cigar.
